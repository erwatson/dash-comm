A communication app for people who can't speak or use their hands.

Introducing Dash Communication - an iPad app created specifically for people who need to communicate the most, but have difficulties doing so.

Launching soon on Kickstarter!

Dash is currently in prototype development and testing in hospitals and beyond. Simply sign up below to find out when we launch on kickstarter so you can help the cause and get the Dash app early at a discount.

Dash makes selecting keywords and "writing" anything with the alphabet simple by breaking everything down into four directional buttons.

Almost all people who can't speak or use their hands can still indicate all four directions: Up, Down, Left, Right. So, we've broken down communication to be that simple.
